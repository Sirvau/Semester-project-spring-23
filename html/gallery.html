Exhibition Gallery

We believe science should not be confined to the textbook, but brought to live through exhibits..any of these exhibits are designed for you to interact with and play around to see science come to life (apart from the dinosaur exhibits, they only come to life at night when everyone is gone home).

Evolution

Come and experience the mysteries of the natural world in the Evolution exhibition room.

Take a journey across the ages and see the skeletons of ancient creatures as you discover how creatures evolved over time.

Cosmology

Explore the grandeur of the cosmos with our Cosmology Exhibition!

Build starships in LEGO tec, visit the universe through virtual reality technology, and view models of the planets.

Robotics and AI

Learn to program and code robots and explore the many possibilities of artificial intelligence.

Come and explore the cutting edge of technology in the Robotics and AI exhibition room.
