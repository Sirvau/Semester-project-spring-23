About

Come join us at the Community Science Museum where we are committed to making science accessible to all!

The aim of our museum is to create a space where everyone can experience the wonders of our discoveries and perhaps even ignite a lifelong passion to continue the exploration of the world around us.

Other museum areas

There is a café attached to the museum where you can get light lunches, soft drinks, coffee, snacks and more.

We also have a play zone where you can bring younger children. Here you will find a little library, toys and have the possibility to color digital drawings on big interactive screens.

There are great outdoor areas around the museum that are suitable for play and fun.
